Ghoul Grinder

Erenia is in peril! The war against the Drow King, Kwua Ven, has reached a turning point. The finest paladins of Emperor Harrad were called to court a week ago. Little did they know they were being called to their dooms! The knights entered the chamber to find their emperor's head upon Kwua Ven's pike! It took only moments for the finest men of Erenia to join the undead legions of the dark lord. You, brave knight, are our only hope against this evil! Take up your sword and grind those ghouls to dust.
